CKB Interiors specializes in the design and construction of millwork and custom cabinetry in the Greater Toronto Area. We're all about unique ideas, customization, affordability and we love it.

Making your dreams, a reality.

To create incomparable living spaces, we specialize in the design and construction of millwork and custom cabinetry projects such as kitchen cabinets, bathroom vanities, built-in closets, libraries, entertainment units and more. Individually designed and created to suit your needs and vision.

With over 20 years of experience, we at CKB Interiors understand the importance of your living space and work closely with you to ensure all your needs are met. Regardless of scale, we promise exceptional living spaces that provide quality, function, and excellent craftsmanship.
